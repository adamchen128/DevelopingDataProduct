Next Word Prediction

This a simple Natural Language Processing Shiny App for predicting the possible words next to the Input Box typing. The training data used for building the predictive model is from https://d396qusza40orc.cloudfront.net/dsscapstone/dataset/Coursera-SwiftKey.zip.



  • Input Box:

    Words entered for predicting the next word.

  • No. of Predicted Words:

    The maximun number of words will be displayed.

  • Predicted Words:

    The predicted next word(s), shown after you click the [Submit] button.

  • Currently Typed Word:

    Will display a posible word if you type a word partially.
